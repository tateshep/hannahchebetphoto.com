About

Birth photography captures one of the most significant events that a family will experience. It is an instance that changes lives forever and is one of the most important and rewarding to document.

My Passion

I am based in Bend Oregon, and feel priveledged to be able to call Central Oregon my home since childhood when I received my first DSLR. It has been an amazing setting in which to practice my art.

Pricing Packages

I offer a variety of pricing options. If you have a photo shoot idea that sounds exciting, I am sure we can come to an agreement that satisifies both of us. Typically I offer three categories; Mini Sessions, Extended Sessions, and all Inclusives.

Features

Our time together will vary depending on package. A mini session is a quick 30 minute photo shoot, resulting in around 20 edited images. Other options range from any amount of time up to the all inclusive 2 hour session, with over 40 edits.
